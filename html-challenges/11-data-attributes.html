Challenge 11: Working with Data Attributes

Task:

Create product cards using custom data attributes to store product information. Click on any product to display its details.

Requirements:

  • Use data-* attributes to store product information
  • Include data for: product ID, name, price, category, and stock status
  • Add JavaScript to read and display the data attributes when clicked
  • Create at least 5 different product cards
